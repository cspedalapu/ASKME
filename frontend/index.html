Our Mission

ASKME is designed to transform university support services by delivering real-time, multilingual, and context-aware responses. It is especially useful for environments with heavy query volume, limited staff bandwidth, and a need for consistent answers across departments.

Why ASKME?

24/7 Academic Support

Always-available answers to student and staff questions, reducing wait time and repetitive workload.

Multilingual and Personalized

Supports multiple languages and adapts responses to the user context when relevant information is available.

Advanced RAG and LLMs

Combines retrieval, reranking, and generative AI to produce more grounded and useful responses.

Analytics and Feedback

Supports logging, evaluation, and continuous improvement across the question-answering workflow.
