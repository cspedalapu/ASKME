ASKME Chat

Our Mission

ASKME is an intelligent GenAI-powered assistant designed to transform university support services. It delivers real-time, context-aware, and multilingual responses to student queries — with a special focus on international student needs — through advanced NLP, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), and fine-tuned Large Language Models (LLMs).

Why ASKME?

24/7 Academic Support

Always-available, AI-driven answers to student and staff questions, reducing wait times and staff workload.

Multilingual & Personalized

Supports multiple languages and adapts responses to individual student needs and context.

Advanced RAG & LLMs

Combines Retrieval-Augmented Generation with state-of-the-art LLMs for accurate, document-level understanding.

Analytics & Feedback

Tracks queries and feedback for continuous improvement and actionable insights.

Who is ASKME for?

Students

Instant answers to academic, administrative, and campus life questions—anytime, anywhere.

Staff

Automates repetitive queries, freeing up time for more complex student support tasks.

Administrators

Provides analytics, feedback, and scalable support for university operations.

Technology & Research

  • Powered by GPT-4o, LLaMA 3.3, and advanced NLP frameworks
  • Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) with ChromaDB
  • Customizable for any academic institution
  • Secure, privacy-focused, and scalable
